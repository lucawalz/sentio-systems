# ADR-0015: Low-Latency Video Streaming via RTMPS

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Wildlife monitoring requires real-time or near real-time video streaming from IoT devices to the dashboard. Many placement locations (like university networks or corporate environments) employ restrictive firewalls that block standard streaming protocols like RTSP or WebRTC on non-standard ports.

## Decision
We will standardize on **RTMPS (RTMP over TLS)** for video ingestion. Devices will stream to a central MediaMTX proxy server using **Port 443** (standard HTTPS port) to ensure maximum compatibility with firewalls and provide encrypted transport for video feeds.

## Consequences
**Positive:**
* Bypasses most firewalls by using Port 443.
* Ensures secure, encrypted video transmission.
* Leverages standard RTMP support in most open-source camera libraries.

**Negative:**
* Slightly higher overhead due to TLS encryption compared to raw RTMP.
* Requires certificate management for the proxy server.
